Women Rising

Status, Trends and Challenges

Women Rising seeks to disseminate facts and trends about women's ascendance to the upper eschelon of business management. Our goal is to provide access to information that clearly communicates the magnitude and impact of female executive management across the globe. While headlines in the United State tout gains women have made progressing through the C-Suite, real female management representation still falls well below that of men. Women Rising presents the gender gaps around the globe both at present and historically. When women succeed we all succeed, both in business and as a community. The goal of this website is to highlight where the fundamental gaps lie and potential directions for narrowing the gender gap.

Basic Water

By 2030, achieve universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all. For basic water target, it means access to clean water with-in walking distance. And for safe water target, it means access to clean water in the home.

Basic Sanitation

By 2030, achieve access to adequate and equitable sanitation and hygiene for all and end open defecation, paying special attention to the needs of women and girls and those in vulnerable situations.

Hygiene

By 2030, achieve access to adequate and equitable sanitation and hygiene for all by implementing handwashing stations or soap and water at home.

Safe Water

By 2030, substantially increase water-use efficiency across all sectors and ensure sustainable withdrawals and supply of freshwater to address water scarcity and substantially reduce the number of people suffering from water scarcity.

Safe Sanitation

By 2030, improve water quality by reducing pollution, eliminating dumping and minimizing release of hazardous chemicals and materials, halving the proportion of untreated wastewater and substantially increasing recycling and safe reuse globally.
